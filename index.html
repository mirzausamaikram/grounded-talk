‚ùì Questions you might have

Will you tell anyone what I said?

Nope. Everything stays between us ‚Äî unless you tell me someone's in danger, the law says I have to, or you mention abuse of a kid. Otherwise it's locked.

Can you just tell me what to do?

Not really my thing. I'm not here to fix your problems or give you answers. But talking it through with someone who actually listens? That usually helps you figure it out yourself.

What language will we use?

Whatever works for you ‚Äî English, Arabic, Urdu, Hindi, or Punjabi. Just let me know when you book.

What if I'm actually in crisis?

This isn't the right fit for that. I'll be straight up about it and point you to actual crisis services. You deserve proper help, not peer listening.

Do I need to "prepare" anything?

Nah. Just show up with whatever's on your mind. Find somewhere quiet. That's it.

What if I get emotional or cry?

That's completely fine‚Äîexpected, even. This is a safe space for real emotions. I've seen it all. You won't make things awkward. Let it out if you need to.

Can I book a session as a gift for a friend?

Yes! Just email me with their name and a way to reach them. I'll contact them directly to schedule. Make sure they're okay with it first though‚Äîsurprises aren't always great with this kind of thing.

Do you take notes during sessions?

Only minimal ones‚Äîkey points to help me remember context if we have another session. I don't record anything. Notes are encrypted and deleted after 3 months. Your privacy matters.

What if I ghost you?

If you don't show up and don't tell me, that session's marked as used. No second chances on that one. But if you're running late or something comes up, just send a message ‚Äî I get it.

Online or in-person?

Both are available. Online is HKD 180, in-person (Hong Kong only) is HKD 280. You pick what works for you.
